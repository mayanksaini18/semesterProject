Overview
*The Monk Who Sold His Ferrari* is a self-help book that tells the story of Julian Mantle, a successful lawyer who leaves behind his materialistic lifestyle in search of purpose and enlightenment. The book shares wisdom about mindfulness, happiness, and achieving a balanced life.
